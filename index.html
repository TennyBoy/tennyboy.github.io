About Me

My name is Tennison Boyce, a 21-year-old media content creator. With passions such as web development and photography, I strive to bring you the best media content on the internet. I have been interested in coding ever since I was 14-years-old, starting with the passion to develop video games, I eventually discovered my love for web development. Since then I have spent my time learning and becoming proficient in coding languages such as HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I have worked on mulitple projects ranging from social media platforms to landing pages for small businesses. I found my passion for photography through my love for cars. I started doing photography inside of video games such as Horizon: Zero Dawn and Need For Speed Heat. Once I got my license at 16 I started driving around and taking pictures of every cool car I saw. I would offer to do free photoshoots for everyone I met until I eventually landed my first clients, a Dodge Charger and Challenger.
