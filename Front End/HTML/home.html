About Me

Hey there! I'm Tennison Boyce, a 21-year-old website developer, photographer, and discord server creater with a passion for crafting the ultimate digital media experience. I've been fascinated with digital media from a young age, always wanting to take that perfect photo, make that perfect post, and create an experience that leaves a lasting impression.

Armed with experience in coding languages such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Python, I have been able to make websites that bring in new users and keep existing members coming back. With experience in photography and editing software similar to Photoshop, I have honed my skills in capturing and editing images to convey the exact mood and message I want to express. My journey as a Discord server creator has allowed me to foster communities where members feel engaged and valued, curating spaces where they can connect and share their passions.

My goal is to continue pushing the boundaries of digital media, exploring new technologies and techniques to create immersive experiences that resonate with audiences. Whether it's through stunning visuals, seamless user interfaces, or engaging community interactions, I'm dedicated to crafting the ultimate digital media experiences that leave a lasting impact.
